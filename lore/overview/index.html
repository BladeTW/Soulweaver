    Lore

    The world has always been filled with invisible forces, though few have ever understood them.

    Every living creature possesses a Soul—an immortal fragment of cosmic energy that records its existence, personality, and purpose. While the body eventually returns to the earth, the soul is destined to continue its journey beyond the mortal world.

    Most souls pass on peacefully.

    Some do not.

    The Two Attunements

    Every soul is naturally aligned with one of two primordial forces:

    • Angel — Souls attuned to harmony, preservation, and order.
    • Devil — Souls attuned to ambition, conflict, and change.

    Neither Attunement is inherently good or evil.

    An Angel soul may fiercely defend its territory, while a Devil soul may simply seek freedom above all else. Attunement influences a soul's instincts rather than its morality.

    This balance exists throughout nature, and both forces are essential for the world to remain stable.

    The Celestial Wheel

    Beyond their Attunement, every soul is marked by one of the Twelve Zodiac Constellations.

    These celestial signs influence how a soul behaves, how it responds to magic, and how compatible it is with other souls.

    Scholars believe the stars constantly weave new souls into existence, imprinting each with a Zodiac before it enters the world.

    The Zodiac cannot normally be changed without powerful rituals.

    Wandering Spirits

    Occasionally, a soul fails to complete its journey after death.

    Instead, it lingers within the mortal realm as a Wandering Spirit.

    These ethereal beings drift across the world carrying an exposed soul at their core.

    Their appearance and behaviour depend entirely on the soul they carry.

    Angel Spirits are peaceful, often avoiding confrontation unless threatened.

    Devil Spirits are aggressive and will actively hunt intruders who wander too close.

    Each Wandering Spirit represents a single soul that can eventually be captured by skilled Soulwrights.

    Unstable Souls

    When a Wandering Spirit is defeated, its body disperses almost instantly.

    The soul, no longer protected by its spiritual shell, becomes Unstable.

    An Unstable Soul can survive only a few moments before collapsing under its own magical energy.

    If left uncaptured, it detonates in a burst of soul energy, permanently destroying itself.

    For this reason, Soulwrights always carry Soul Jars when hunting spirits.

    Soulwrights

    Very few individuals possess the knowledge required to manipulate souls safely.

    Those who do are known as Soulwrights.

    Rather than controlling life itself, Soulwrights study the magical properties of souls, learning how to preserve, combine, breed, and bind them into powerful constructs.

    Their workshops are filled with strange multiblock machines, celestial mirrors, ritual circles, and artificial servants powered by captured souls.

    To outsiders, their work appears miraculous.

    To a Soulwright, it is simply another branch of natural magic.

    Golems

    A soul can be bound into an artificial body through the Golem Forge, giving rise to a Golem.

    Unlike mindless automatons, every Golem possesses a distinct personality inherited from the soul within.

    A Golem's:

    • Behaviour
    • Combat style
    • Abilities
    • Temperament

    are all influenced by the soul bound inside it.

    As Soulwrights become more experienced, they learn to bind multiple souls into a single construct, creating companions of incredible power.

    However, combining incompatible souls risks catastrophic instability.

    Instability

    Souls were never intended to share the same vessel.

    When multiple souls are bound together, their Attunements and Zodiac influences begin competing for control.

    Compatible souls coexist peacefully.

    Incompatible souls slowly destabilise the construct.

    If instability grows too great, the Golem loses its identity entirely.

    The souls fuse into a twisted consciousness, transforming the construct into a violent Cursed Spirit that attacks everything nearby—including its creator.

    Many ancient ruins are believed to house the remains of experiments that ended this way.

    Starlight

    The stars are more than distant lights.

    They are conduits of celestial energy that continuously shape the world's spiritual balance.

    Using specially constructed Mirror Arrays, Soulwrights can focus this energy onto ordinary Redstone, transforming it into Soul Powder.

    Soul Powder forms the foundation of nearly every advanced ritual, making access to the open night sky essential for any successful workshop.

    During rare celestial events, the stars burn brighter, greatly increasing the amount of Soul Powder that can be collected.

    The Balance

    Neither Angel nor Devil may dominate the world.

    Too much Angelic influence leads to stagnation, where nothing changes.

    Too much Devil influence brings unchecked chaos and destruction.

    The world's spiritual health depends upon the delicate balance between these opposing forces.

    Every soul captured, every ritual performed, and every Golem awakened shifts that balance ever so slightly.

    As a Soulwright, your choices will determine not only the fate of your workshop, but perhaps the fate of the world itself.
